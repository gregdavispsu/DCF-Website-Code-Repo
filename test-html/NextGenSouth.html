About Next Gen South

Overview

  • Established in 2012, Next Gen South is building an endowed fund at the DCF to generate money for charitable grants for decades to come.
  • In October 2013, Next Gen South awarded $12,500 in grants.
  • Next Gen South is currently focused on supporting mental health services for children.
