IRA Charitable Rollover Extension: Deadline Jan. 31!
Take Advantage of Tax Benefits for 2012 and 2013.

The Charitable IRA Rollover allows individuals age 70 ½ and older to donate up to $100,000 per year to public charities tax free from their Individual Retirement Accounts (IRAs).

First enacted in 2006, the provision was extended for 2012 and 2013 as part of the American Taxpayer Relief Act of 2012.

 

Transfer up to $200,000 tax free!

The extension of the Charitable IRA Rollover means you can transfer up to $100,000 per person ($200,000 for a couple) to your existing or new charitable fund at the Delaware Community Foundation. Funds are sent directly to the charitable fund and are never counted as income, so you avoid paying income tax.

(Although donor advised funds are not covered by this provision, the DCF can help donor-advised fundholders identify ways to enjoy the full benefits of this opportunity. Please contact us to discuss.)

You have until Jan. 31, 2013, to:

  • Make a new transfer directly from your IRA, or
  • Transfer automatic payouts you received in December 2012.

The news comes just as 2013 tax-deduction limits go down for individuals earning more than $400,000 per year and couples earning $450,000 or more. Who could this benefit most? Seniors who are required to take an IRA payout they don't need.

Leverage your highest-taxed assets.

Retirement distributions are some of the most highly taxed forms of income. A charitable IRA rollover can benefit Delaware seniors in two ways:

  • Avoid the federal income tax you would normally incur on an IRA payout.
  • Reduce your state taxes by decreasing your Delaware taxable income.

How can you take advantage of this opportunity?

Contact David Fleming today at 302.504.5224.

  • We will work with you or your financial advisor to arrange a rollover from your IRA to an existing fund at the DCF to maximize your tax benefits.
  • We can help you establish a new fund if you don't already have one. Through your new fund, you can take advantage of these tax benefits, and you can support the causes you care about most -- even if you haven't decided yet what charitable causes you want to support.

Why give through the DCF?

It's permanent. Unlike a one-time charitable gift, a fund at the Delaware Community Foundation -- perhaps named after you or a loved one -- will create funds to support your favorite causes forever.

It's easy. At the DCF, we are experts in charitable funds and in charitable organizations. We can guide you through your options to help you make the most of your gift.

It's local. We know Delaware's nonprofit sector inside-out. If you're not sure yet how you want to focus your charitable dollars, we can offer ideas and introduce you to organizations doing work that matches your interests.
