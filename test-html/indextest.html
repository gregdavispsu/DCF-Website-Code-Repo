At the Delaware Community Foundation, we connect people who care with the causes they care about, helping individuals and organizations create charitable funds that become permanent sources of funding for charitable causes. We also:

  • - Inspire and expand philanthropy
  • - Empower nonprofit organizations
  • - Maximize the power of giving
  • - Engage future leaders in philanthropy

At the DCF, we make it easy for generous people to do great things for the First State. Contact us for help figuring out what your gift can do for Delaware.

Apply for Grants to Provide Child Mental Health Services in Kent & Sussex

Applications are now available for 2014 grants from The Next Generation of Southern Delaware! Organizations providing children's mental health services in Kent and Sussex Counties are invited to apply.

Last year, Next Gen South awarded $12,500 in grants to three nonprofit organizations providing programs to benefit children’s mental health.

-- La Esperanza received $5,000 to help support its Victims Services Program, which provides counseling for Latino children who are victims of domestic violence and/or sexual abuse in Sussex County.

-- Delaware Guidance Services received $5,000 to help support therapy sessions for at-risk children in Sussex County.

-- Girl Scouts of the Chesapeake Bay received $2,500 to help support its bully prevention “Be a Friend First” (BFF) program to high-risk girls in Kent County.
