About Next Gen North

Overview

  • Established in 2004, Next Gen North is building an endowed fund at the DCF to generate money for charitable grants for decades to come.
  • Over the past nine years, Next Gen North has awarded more than $280,000 in grants in support of initiatives that address underlying social challenges faced by the most vulnerable Delawareans.
  • Next Gen North is currently focused on supporting programs that promote Science, Technology, Engineering & Mathematics education.

Events

Below is a brief overview of the events we hold throughout the year:

Monthly Meetings - The second Wednesday of every month at 5:30 p.m. in the Community Service Building.

Grants Ceremony â In February the hard work from the year before pays off with our Grants Ceremony. Drinks and hors d’oeuvres are served as we give away grants to several deserving nonprofits.

Preview Party - Sneak a peak at Chips for Charity during a happy hour at a local watering hole, complete with opportunities to win prizes and tickets to the main event.

Chips for Charity â Chips for Charity is an annual high-class casino night at the Blue Ball Barn, the first Saturday of March. The event boasts table games and dealers, an open bar, catering, a photo booth, a silent auction, 50/50, raffle prizes, a DJ and much more! Unlike other events, you exchange your chips for raffle tickets which put you in the running for big ticket prizes at the end of the night.

Fall Family Festival â Fall Family Festival is held annually at Ramsey’s Farm on the first Saturday in October. Families come out to enjoy an afternoon of pumpkin painting, hay rides, pony rides, face painting, food, ice cream, live music and a few drinks. Silent auction and 50/50 are available, as is the corn maze at Ramsey Farm!

Social Events â Next Gen North hosts numerous social events throughout the year. Next Gen has a work hard, play hard mentality, which lends itself well to the demographics of the group. Generally, there will be a social after Chips for Charity to make sure the leftover alcohol does not go to waste, a wine/beer tasting at Premier Liquors (our official liquor sponsor), as well as an array of activities to support our sister organization, Next Gen South.
