About Next Gen South

Overview

  • Established in 2008, Next Gen-South is building an endowed fund at the DCF to generate money for charitable grants for decades to come.
  • In the summer of 2009, Next Gen-South will award its inaugural grants.
  • Next Gen-South is currently focused on supporting mental health services for children.
