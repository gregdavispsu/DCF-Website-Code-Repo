Grant Applications for Review 2013

Dear Committee Members,

Thank you for participating in the grant-selection process. Below are PDFs of the grants for your review, broken down by groups and by field of interest. All applications are accessible to all members, regardless of group, and you are welcome to read as many as wish.

Access Your Grant Applications

Please go to your group's list and download the applications to review.

  • By clicking the first link under your group, you will download ALL of your group's applications as a single PDF file. Before you print, please note that these are very long files.
  • If you prefer to download individual applications, click the appropriate links.

Evaluation Process

  • Download the Application Evaluation Guidelines.
  • Download the scoring sheet, which is unique to your group.
  • Save it as a file named in this format: ScoreSheet-GroupNumber-YourLastName.doc (e.g., ScoreSheet-1-Smith.doc).
  • Download, review and score your group's applications on the single scoring sheet.
  • Email the single scoring sheet to me at bbouchelle@delcf.org by March 15.
  • Please title the email "Scores-GroupNumber-YourLastName" (e.g., Scores-1-Smith).

Abstentions

If you need to abstain from voting due to a personal or professional affiliation with the applicant organization, on the scoresheet, please indicate AB as the total score in that line of the spreadsheet.

Please contact me if you have any questions.

Thank you!
Beth
