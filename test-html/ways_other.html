Other Ways To Support The DCF
DCF Friends Fund
Gifts to the Delaware Community Foundation from individuals, families, businesses and organizations support our efforts to make Delaware a better place to live and work. Each year, our Friends Fund provides the funding for the human and financial resources we need to help our donors achieve their philanthropic goals. A gift to our Friends Fund supports our infrastructure and operations, secures our future and helps us carry out our mission. This past year's gifts helped us provide better services for seniors, introduce young people to philanthropy and encourage nonprofits throughout the state to work more closely together. If you believe in the work we do in supporting the people who care, who in turn support the causes they care about, we hope you will make a generous contribution to the DCF's Friends Fund. To make a donation, click here.

2014 DCF Corporate Partners Fund
For more than 25 years, businesspeople have helped the Delaware Community Foundation lay the groundwork for a better Delaware. Their steadfast contributions through our Corporate Partners Fund campaigns have enabled us to help local nonprofit organizations identify and meet critical needs throughout our state. Your participation sends a clear message to others about the importance of building a stronger philanthropic culture and permanent source of charitable funds for the First State.

2014 DCF Corporate Sponsors Program
Each year the Delaware Community Foundation hosts a number of events and creates communication vehicles to build awareness about the work of the Foundation and the people who care who are involved with us. Sponsorship opportunities are available for our planned giving webinars and annual receptions; for our quarterly newsletter, annual report and our website. Varying levels of sponsorships are available as well as recognition of your contribution.

To learn more about the Delaware Community Foundation's Friends Fund or our Corporate Sponsor and Corporate Partners Programs and how you can become a contributor, please contact Amelia Welch-Wierzbicki, Director of Development by calling 302.504.5230 or email awierzbicki@delcf.org. Gifts may be sent to the Delaware Community Foundation, P.O. Box 1636, Wilmington, DE 19899.
